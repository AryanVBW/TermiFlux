TermiFlux

Seamlessly switch between ARM64 and x86_64 shell on your Apple Silicon Mac

Quick Installation

Copy and paste this command in your terminal to install TermiFlux:

curl -sSL https://raw.githubusercontent.com/AryanVBW/TermiFlux/main/install_TermiFlux.sh | bash

Shell Selection

After installation, you'll be prompted to select your preferred shell architecture:

After selecting an architecture and launching, you may get the option menu again. Don't worry - just select option 3 (exit) for the second time and you're ready to go. We're working on fixing this behavior.
