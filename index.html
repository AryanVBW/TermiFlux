TermiFlux

Seamlessly switch between ARM64 and x86_64 shell on your Apple Silicon Mac

Quick Installation

1Install TermiFlux

Copy and paste this command in your terminal:

/bin/bash -c "$(curl -fsSL https://raw.githubusercontent.com/AryanVBW/TermiFlux/refs/heads/main/install_TermiFlux.sh)"

2Activate TermiFlux

Choose your shell type and run the appropriate command:

For Zsh (Default shell):
source ~/.zshrc
For Bash:
source ~/.bashrc

3Launch Terminal

Open a new terminal window and you'll automatically get options to choose your architecture:

Note: After selecting an architecture and launching, you may get the option menu again. Don't worry - just select option 3 (exit) for the second time and you're ready to go. We're working on fixing this behavior.
